[Listen to Episode 5: The Future of Indians on *Domination Chronicles*.](https://dominationchronicles.com/episodes/e005-future-of-indians/)

A conversation sparked by Vine Deloria Jr.'s 1975 article and a 2025 law review article on questions troubling the hosts.

This conversation takes up a question that has never been merely academic. What future is imagined when federal law continues to define Native peoples through domination, dependency, and property-centered assumptions?

I am sharing this episode here because it belongs with my wider work on the language of domination, Christian discovery, federal Indian law, and the continuing reality of Original Nations' free existence. Each conversation in *Domination Chronicles* is another way into that inquiry.
